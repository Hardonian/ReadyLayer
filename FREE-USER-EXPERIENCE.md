## FREE USER ANXIETY POINTS

### Anxiety Point 1: High Issues Slip Through
**When:** Week 3-4  
**Feeling:** "Warnings feel risky, wish they were blocked"  
**Impact:** High issues cause bugs, incidents  
**Resolution:** Upgrade to Growth tier (high issues blocked)

### Anxiety Point 2: No Pattern Detection
**When:** Month 2-3  
**Feeling:** "Same issues keep happening, no escalation"

**When:** Month 2-3

**When:** Month 2-3

**Resolution:** Upgrade to Growth tier ($100/month) or Scale tier (unlimited)
