## GROWTH TIER ($199/month)

### What Users Stop Worrying About

#### 1. High Issues Causing Incidents ✅
**Before (Free Tier):**
- High issues are warnings only (not blocked)
- PRs merge with high issue warnings
- High issues cause production bugs
- "I wish ReadyLayer had blocked that"

**After (Growth Tier):**
- High issues are blocked by default
- PRs cannot merge with high issues
- High issues never reach production
- "ReadyLayer blocks high issues, feels safer"

**Value:** Prevent high issue incidents ($50k+ per incident)
